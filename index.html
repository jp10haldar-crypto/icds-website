✨ Features

Everything an Anganwadi
worker needs

All your monthly paperwork, done automatically. No typing errors, no calculation mistakes.

📝

Smart Daily Entry

Dates, days, and menus are pre-filled automatically. You only enter attendance counts and any new stock received.

📦

Automatic Stock Tracking

Opening stock auto-fills from last month's closing. Stock balance updates daily as you enter attendance — no manual calculation.

📊

One-Click Monthly Report

Tap Generate Report and get a complete report with attendance totals, consumption, stock balance, and spending breakdown.

🍱

Menu Chart Editor

View and edit the Menu A / Menu B nutrition rates (Rice, Pulses, Oil, Vegetable, Potato, Egg) per person type.

🔒

No Future Month Entry

The app blocks future months from being selected, preventing accidental entry and keeping your records accurate.

☁️

Cloud Saved — Always Safe

All data is saved to the cloud via Firebase. Change phone, log back in — all your history is right there.

From daily entry
to complete report

1

Select Month & Enter Opening Stock

Pick the month. If you generated last month's report, opening stock fills in automatically from closing stock.

2

Enter Daily Attendance

Each day, enter Mother count and Children count. Menu is pre-assigned (A or B alternating), Sundays marked Off automatically.

3

Log Stock Received (When Needed)

On any day new stock arrives, enter Rice/Pulses/Oil received. The running balance updates immediately.

4

Generate Monthly Report

At month end, tap Generate Report. The app calculates everything and saves the closing stock for next month.
